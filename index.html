The Reality

Every business in India runs on relationships. On memory. On the chai that gets made when the right person walks in. But memory has limits — and limits compound quietly. The customer you forgot to follow up with chose someone else. The process that only you understand cannot survive a single bad week. The insight that could have changed your direction lived and died inside a WhatsApp thread. This is not a technology problem. It never was. It is an infrastructure problem — the kind that does not announce itself until the gap between you and the businesses that got it right becomes impossible to close.
